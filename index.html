The journey and my learnings.

As a millenial having no prior experience in coding, I write about my experience of learning to code in my 30s.

Surprisingly, the biggest obstacle that I faced (and still face, although less frequently) was my own self-doubt and my mental block towards learning to program.

"Can you break into tech in your 30s?"

"Is a career change even possible at this age?"

"Would I be able to learn to code?"

"Do I need to be a genius to do this?"

"Is it too late??!"

These were some of the nagging doubts that kept me from progressing in the early months of learning to code.

Now 12 months into my learning journey, I write about what I discovered about myself while pursuing my goals.

If you are reading this, you are most likely pursuing a similar path, and I hope that sharing my thoughts will help you in your journey as well.

Best of luck!
